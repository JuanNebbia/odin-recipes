Puré de papas

Description

This is definitely one of the most popular options to accompany any dish in Argentina. It is a very simple recipe, but if you know some tricks it can go from 'meh' to 'wow' instantly.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium sized potatoes
  • 50 gr. of butter
  • 100 cc. of milk
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • a pinch of nutmeg
  • a pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes in regular dices. Keep in mind that the boiling time will depend of the dice's size.
  2. Boil the potatoes dices and the garlic cloves in water with salt.
  3. Once the potato is soft, turn off the stove and remove most of the water.
  4. Add the nutmeg, the butter and the milk and smash everything with a potato masher (or, failing that, a fork)
  5. If it gets too liquid turn on the stove again for a little while.
  6. Once you reach the consistency you like it is ready to eat!
  7. Now you have the perfect accompaniment for a great milanesa
  8. Optional: You can add some cheese to the mix if you like
  9. Optional 2: If you prefer a chewy consistency you should use a mixer intead of a potato masher
