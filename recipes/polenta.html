Polenta

Description

Nothing is more comforting than a good polenta in a rainy day. This meal is made with corn flour and prepared in a huge different ways.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of precooked polenta
  • 3 cups of water or milk
  • 50 gr of butter
  • 100 gr of fresh cheese
  • a pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Boil the water (or milk) whit the salt in a cooking pot
  2. When starts boiling, turn down the stove and slowly add the polenta while stirring, to avoid lumps.
  3. Turn off the stove and add the butter and the cheese.
  4. Stir until cheese melts.
  5. And simple as that, you have polenta ready to eat.
  6. Tip: although you can eat it just like that, it is common to add some tomato based sauce, such as bolognese.
