Milanesa

Description

Milanesa is one of the most loved dishes in Argentina, people of all ages worship this delicacy. They can be made of beef, chicken, fish or vegetables and their breading also accepts different combinations such as bread, corn or oatmeal. Here we will see the most traditional option, with beef and breadcrumbs

Ingredients

  • 4 beef steak
  • 300 gr. breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • a bunch fresh parsley
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • salt

Steps

  1. Finely chop the parsley and the garlic cloves.
  2. Beat the eggs and add the parley, the garlic and a pinch of salt.
  3. Introduce the steaks into the mixture until they are completely covered.
  4. Coat the steak in breadcrumbs.
  5. Place the steaks on an oiled baking dish.
  6. Cook in the oven previously heated at medium temperature for 20 minutes.
  7. That's it! you can accompany it with smashed potatoes or a tomato-lettuce salad.
  8. Optional: You can choose to fry the milanesas too. In that case you could repeat steps 3 and 4 twice and cook them in hot oil.
